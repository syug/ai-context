## Important Note: IKEA King Size vs Australian King Size

IKEA uses European sizing. Their "King" beds are **180 x 200 cm**, while the Australian standard King is **183 x 203 cm**. This is a 3 cm difference in both width and length. Implications:
- An Australian King mattress (183x203) will NOT fit properly in an IKEA King frame (180x200). It will overhang or need to be forced in.
- You MUST buy an IKEA-sized mattress (180x200) to match the frame, or accept a slight gap with a smaller mattress.
- Standard Australian King fitted sheets may be slightly loose on IKEA-sized mattresses.

Source: IKEA product pages list mattress size as 180x200cm; OzBargain users confirm the sizing mismatch.

## Delivery Options to Surry Hills 2010

- **Parcel delivery** (small items under 250 kg total): from $9.99 (or $5.99 for IKEA Family members on orders over $100)
- **Truck delivery** (larger items): from $19
- Delivery to door/building entrance only
- 12-hour delivery window
- **Does NOT include carrying upstairs** -- left at ground floor entrance

### Delivery Into Home
- **From $49** -- items carried into ONE room of your choice
- 6-hour delivery window
- **Important question:** It is unclear whether "into home" delivery covers 3rd floor walk-up apartments. Standard practice is delivery to the first accessible room. For walk-ups, drivers may refuse or charge extra. Worth confirming with IKEA before ordering.
- Weekend surcharge: $5 extra (NSW)

### Click & Collect
- Available at IKEA Tempe (closest to Surry Hills)
- Free if picking up yourself
- Would require a vehicle large enough for 200+ cm packages

- IKEA partners with GoGet for van rentals

## Assembly Service

### IKEA Assembly Service
- **Starting from $40** (call-out fee)
- Pricing based on time to assemble
- Typical bed frame assembly: estimated $80-$150 (not officially quoted for beds specifically, but based on comparable items like $120 for a PAX wardrobe)
- **Book during checkout** (online or in-store)
- Provider: Third-party professional partners (not TaskRabbit in Australia)

### What Assembly Service Includes:
- Professional assembly per IKEA instructions
- Wall attachment where required by safety instructions
- Assumes clear, level space with 50 cm clearance around flat packages

### What Assembly Service Does NOT Include:
- **Moving packages to assembly room** -- they assemble where the boxes are
- Packaging removal (extra $15, must be booked with assembly)
- Electrical work
- Product modifications

### TaskRabbit
- TaskRabbit is NOT officially available through IKEA Australia (unlike US/UK)
- However, TaskRabbit does operate in Sydney independently -- you could hire a Tasker for assembly
- Typical Tasker rates for furniture assembly: $40-$80/hour
- Advantage: Taskers will often help carry items upstairs too

### Airtasker (Australian Alternative)
- Popular in Sydney for furniture assembly
- Typical rates: $30-$70/hour
- Can often negotiate a package deal for carrying boxes upstairs + assembly
- Search "IKEA bed assembly Surry Hills" on Airtasker for quotes

## Recommendation for 3rd Floor Walk-Up

**Best overall for stairs + quality: HEMNES King ($699)**
- Lightest total weight (60.6 kg)
- No package over 28 kg (manageable for one fit person, comfortable for two)
- Solid pine construction survives multiple moves
- 10-year IKEA guarantee
- Classic design that doesn't look "budget"
- Only 5 packages to carry up

**Best budget option: MALM High King ($499)**
- Very similar package weights and dimensions to HEMNES
- $200 cheaper
- But particleboard won't survive as many moves
- Slightly more prone to squeaking

**Avoid for stairs: BRIMNES with Headboard ($799)**
- Headboard package alone is 38.4 kg
- Total 151.6 kg across 7 packages
- Assembly is complex and time-consuming
