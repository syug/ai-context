## 1. Pause Ads

### 1.1 Product Overview

A static ad that appears when a viewer pauses streaming. Supports remote-enabled interactivity ("Add to Cart" for endemic, "Send to Email" for non-endemic brands). Full-screen takeover on living room devices (FireTV and 3P LR devices). Not available on mobile/web/desktop.

## 2. First Impression Takeover (FITO)

### 2.1 Product Overview

A scaled takeover of the first pre-roll slot across Prime Video's catalog. The brand's ad is the first one a customer sees during their viewing session that day. Flat fee pricing model. Non-guaranteed delivery (impressions are estimated). Frequency capped at 1x per day. Does NOT serve against sponsored titles, TNF, or live content.

## 3. Interactive Ads (IVA)

### 3.1 Product Overview

Interactive Video Advertising (IVA) transforms video ads into actionable experiences. Viewers can interact via remote or QR code to add products to cart or learn more. **IVA is a default opt-in feature** for non-3PAS Streaming TV and PVA ads. Available across all Prime Video content types at no additional cost.

### 3.2 Available Details from This Page

The product deep-dive page for Interactive Ads is minimal -- it primarily redirects to the full [IVA wiki](https://w.amazon.com/bin/view/AdSales/GVA/Products/InteractiveVideoAds). Key points:

- **Pricing:** No additional cost (included with Prime Video campaigns where 1P serving is possible)
- **Availability:** Across all Prime Video content types
- **Strongly encouraged** for all Prime Video campaigns with 1P video serving
- Supports both remote interaction and QR code
- Two CTA types: "Add to Cart" (endemic), "Learn More / Send to Email" (non-endemic)

### 3.3 Cross-reference: IVA Capabilities from Pause Ads & FITO Pages

From the Pause Ads and FITO pages, we can extract:

**IVA with Pause Ads:**
- Available in all Pause Ad locales (US, EU5, BR, CA, MX, NL/SE, AU, JP; NZ Q4'26)
- Remote interactivity only (no QR code on Pause Ads)
- Two types: "Add to Cart" (endemic) and "Send to Email" (non-endemic)
- Must check [interactivity eligibility](https://w.amazon.com/bin/view/AdSales/GVA/Products/InteractiveVideoAds/Eligibility)

**IVA with FITO:**
- Supported for US and EU only (not available in CA, MX, AU, JP for FITO)

### 4.4 Key Takeaways for TEX/APAC Strategy

1. **AU is the most advanced APAC market** with both Pause Ads and FITO live, but Pause Ads commercial details need urgent definition.

2. **JP has products available but is commercially underdeveloped** -- Pause Ads launched 30-March with all details TBD, and FITO status is ambiguous (3P tracking approved but not listed as GA).

3. **NZ is the next APAC launch** (Pause Ads Q4'26), but FITO is not on the NZ roadmap.

4. **IN, SG, and MENA have zero presence** across all three Innovation products. These represent whitespace opportunities but would require product expansion investment.

5. **IVA on FITO is US/EU only** -- expanding this to APAC would require product development.

6. **3P measurement/tracking gap** is a significant sales barrier for AU and JP Pause Ads, where advertiser expectations for independent verification are high.

7. **No audience or contextual targeting** exists for Pause Ads in any locale, and FITO has no targeting at all. This limits premium/precision selling in all markets.
