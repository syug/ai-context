### 2. First Impression Takeover (FITO)

**Key details:**
- **Available locales**: US, EU5, AU, CA, MX (GA since April/May 2025), JP (listed as available)
- **Pricing (Flat Fee):**
  - US: $300K weekday / $400K weekend
  - UK: GBP66K weekday / GBP76K weekend
  - DE: EUR99K weekday / EUR99K weekend
  - FR: EUR38K weekday / EUR43K weekend
  - IT: EUR43K weekday / EUR48K weekend
  - ES: EUR50K weekday / EUR57K weekend
  - CA: $60K CAD weekday / $65K CAD weekend
  - MX: $400K MXN weekday / $450K MXN weekend
  - **AU: $32K AUD weekday / $38K AUD weekend**
- **Estimated impressions (not guaranteed):**
  - US: 5.9M-8M weekday / 7.6M-10.3M weekend
  - **AU: 403K-448K weekday / 479K-533K weekend**
- Frequency cap: 1x per day at deal level
- Targeting: N/A (run of service)
- **3P Tracking**: Approved for US, CA, MX, BR, JP, AU (NOT EU)
- **3P Measurement**: Approved for US, CA, MX, BR, JP, AU (NOT EU except limited vendors)
- New pricing incentives for buying multiple FITOs on one IO
- SLA: IO 10 BD, Creative 5 BD, Trafficking 3 BD, Complete 1 BD before launch
- IVA (Interactive Video) can run with FITO (currently US and EU only)
- Does NOT serve across TNF / live content
- Does NOT support day-parting (runs full 24 hours)

### 3. Interactive Video Ads (IVA)

**Key details:**
- Default opt-in feature for all non-3PAS PVa and STV ads
- No additional cost to enable
- Available: US, EU5, BR, CA, MX
- Two CTA types: "Add to Cart" (endemic) and QR code / "Learn More" (non-endemic)
- Must check interactivity eligibility for specific advertiser
- Detailed information on dedicated IVA wiki
