## Key Findings

All 3 ideas rated **Yellow** (conditionally feasible). Each has 1 Red blocker, all pivotable:

| Idea | Rating | Red Blocker | Pivot |
|------|--------|-------------|-------|
| 01: Cat Love Keyboard | Yellow | Amazon Music UGC pipeline doesn't exist | Decouple UGC from playlist; pre-curated approach |
| 02: Adore Des Chats | Yellow | Physical product regulatory (APVMA 6-12mo) + manufacturing | Brand owns manufacturing; limited seeding |
| 03: Dinner and a Show | Yellow/Red | Ultrasonic audio impossible on smartphones | Reframe to "calming soundscapes" in 8-18kHz range |

**Recommended priority (tech):** Idea 01 > Idea 03 > Idea 02

## Important Decisions

- Agency "pspspsuedoscience" concept was rejected by BIL — but its DNA lives on in Idea 03 (second-screen audio during PV viewing), reframed as a companion experience rather than embedded audio track
- All 3 ideas share the "12-Day Dine Meal Challenge" as commercial anchor
- The 2026 strategic shift is from "interpreting" (Cat Decoder) to "enabling" emotional connections beyond mealtime
