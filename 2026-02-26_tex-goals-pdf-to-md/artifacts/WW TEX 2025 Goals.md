# WW TEX 2025 Goals

This document outlines TEX's strategic initiatives and their respective finalized goals for FY25. The detailed 3-year strategy and proposed goal list can be found in the FY25 TEX Roadmap.

---

## Product Innovation & Incubation

In 2024, TEX established itself as a critical partner in product development through successful prototypes that influenced Amazon Live's international expansion and CreativeX's GenAI roadmap. These efforts generated $8.8MM in attached revenue while creating new advertising opportunities within Amazon's canvas. For 2025, deepening our product incubation capabilities is essential for maintaining BIL's competitive advantage and ability to shape future advertising experiences. This focus on innovation ensures TEX remains at the forefront of emerging technologies while creating opportunities for brands to connect with customers in novel ways.

- **(VP - Kingpin #918907)** Deliver at least 1 prototype showcasing forward-thinking second-screen experience with advertising applicability for BIL campaigns by EOY. [Maria Bresser]
- **(Team)** Create a KPI and DSI framework to recognize the impact of prototypes and forward-looking innovations resulting in a new BIL Page 0 metric by EOQ2. [Alex Mejias]
- **(Team)** Deliver a doc defining BIL Product Incubation framework and mental models by EOQ2. [Maria Bresser]

---

## Design System & Efficiency

With 60% of campaigns falling within the T2-T3 category in 2024, TEX must revolutionize how these experiences are created to free capacity for strategic work. Currently, designers rely heavily on development resources even for standard implementations, creating bottlenecks and limiting innovation time. By investing in designer empowerment through improved tools and systems, we'll dramatically reduce development needs while maintaining creative excellence. This shift is crucial for scaling BIL's impact and ensuring our most versatile technical resources can focus on breakthrough innovations.

- **(VP - Kingpin #918904)** Launch a tool enabling designers to independently create BIL's low- to medium-complexity (T2 - T3) landing pages and deliver at least 25% of BIL's T2 - T3 campaigns in Q4 without developer support. [Alex Mejias]
- **(Team)** Launch tailored component design kit with consistent grid elements and migrate 10 most-used Halfpipe components to new design standards by EOY. [Juhee Kim]

---

## Operational Excellence & Resource Management

In 2024, TEX devoted 90% of work hours to campaigns, leaving minimal time for strategic initiatives and innovation. This imbalance creates risk for long-term growth and team development. By implementing robust resource management and communication systems, we can better protect time for strategic work while maintaining efficient campaign delivery. These operational improvements will help TEX balance immediate business needs with long-term innovation, ensuring sustained success across our global presence.

- **(Team)** Implement forward-looking TEX resourcing mechanism across major BIL locales by Q2 [Mirko Cappai]
- **(Team)** Establish communications SOP for TEX by EOQ3 [Mirko Cappai]
- **(Team)** Increase coding productivity by 5% through the use of approved generative coding tools [Fitz Maro]

---

## Customer Understanding & Experience Design

TEX and BIL score 5% and 4% below benchmark respectively on understanding customer needs, limiting our ability to create truly customer-obsessed experiences. This gap represents both a challenge and opportunity - by systematizing our approach to user testing and insights gathering, we can transform intuition-based design decisions into data-driven improvements. This foundation of customer understanding will not only improve campaign performance but also strengthen TEX's ability to advocate for customer needs in product and campaign development.

- **(Team)** Launch WW BIL Usertesting mechanism for interactive experiences by EOQ2 [Cyndi Walker]
- **(Team)** Partner with BIL Strategists to launch Insight Repository tool by EOQ2 [Kingyin Cheung]
- **(Team)** Define benchmarks for common UX patterns and implement improvements by EOQ2 [Cyndi Walker]
- **(Team)** Increase customer insights connections score to +5% above benchmark [Kingyin Cheung / BIL-E BAM]

---

## Innovation Culture

While TEX's hackathons represent only 2% of annual time investment, they've produced some of our most impactful tools and features, including A11yAI, Imagainator, and TalkTrack. This demonstrates the immense value of dedicated innovation time and the importance of fostering a culture that encourages bold experimentation. By creating structured programs to celebrate both successes and educational failures, we'll build an environment that consistently drives breakthrough solutions while developing our team's capabilities.

- **(Team)** Launch mechanism to reward failure in experimental initiatives by EOY [Fitz Maro]
- **(Team)** Launch Hackathon DSI framework and conduct three hackathons with partner teams by EOY [Fitz Maro]
