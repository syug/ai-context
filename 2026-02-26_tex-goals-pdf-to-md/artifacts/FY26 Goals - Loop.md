# TEX FY26 Goals

---

## 1. Innovation Pipeline & Partner Incubation

In FY25, TEX delivered 37 prototypes, with 17 pitched and sold into campaigns and 7 pitched but not sold -- demonstrating strong conversion of innovation into revenue. We completed our second-screen VP goal and led our most successful hackathon with the Prime Video team. For 2026, we're evolving from ad-hoc innovation to a systematic pipeline that operationalizes discoveries into product roadmaps, targeting at least 2 prototypes progressing to publisher roadmap commitments across PV, Alexa, and FireTV. We're also applying our innovation capabilities to partner teams -- helping Twitch unlock new sponsor inventory, build measurement infrastructure, and expand Live Sports offerings.

### Core Goal

- **(L10 - Kingpin #1078451 - Owner: Alex)** Deliver at least 2 innovative ad experiences by building and operationalizing an innovation pipeline between Prime Video's Ad-Tech & Monetization team (ATM), Alexa and FireTV's Devices Content & Ads team. [Mappings: amjias (FTV), rawcur (Alexa), mirkocap (FTV)]

### Team Goal

- **(Prototyping - Owner: Fitz)** Deliver 2 prototypes per DT per quarter (measured by PARC activity) -- Each Design Technologist will deliver 2 prototypes per quarter documented in PARC with business rationale. Success will be measured through PARC totals and supported by WBR update tracking and semi-annual audits (mid-year and EOY talent reviews). This 8-prototypes-per-year target aligns with current DT team capacity as we pivot towards only doing T0-T1 campaign builds. Every prototype must demonstrate strategic alignment -- the "why" -- with pod business priorities or provide a technical solution to an identified business opportunity, moving from reactive to proactive innovation. Prototypes will be delivered on a quarterly cadence with visibility in WBR updates and reviews during quarterly pod lead meetings.

### Twitch Partner Goals (TEX Owners: Trevor, Yuvika)

- **(Kingpin #1091732)** Unified Metrics Hub for BPS Campaign: Establish a unified metrics hub for BPS campaigns that includes both performance and sentiment tracking. Aggregate elements such as sentiment tracking, engagement ratios, and 1P data to deepen understanding of campaigns while mitigating risk on Twitch. Launch LCS enablement by 9/8/2026. [Owner: luvic]
- **(Kingpin #1078465)** Drive incremental, native, exclusive sponsor inventory from undermonetized Twitch features: Identify at least 5 3P Twitch Extensions and accelerate to at least 5 BPS-owned Extension formats to launch the "Preferred Extensions Program" -- an exclusive network of sponsorable, net-new custom inventory on Twitch. [Owner: kmccagg]
- **(Kingpin #1078466)** Elevate Twitch's position and program offering within Live Sports: Expand Twitch Creator Casts to at least 1 additional league through PV sports rights and pilot at least 1 second-tier direct live sports partnership. Target 75% sellthrough for 26/27 seasons across Twitch Sports league offerings. [Owner: kmccagg]

## 2. AI-Powered Operational Transformation

TEX's Q3 2025 survey showed 94% of technical team members using AI tools daily, with 50% reporting 30%+ productivity gains. Our Fal.AI pilot demonstrated 98 design hours saved in just one day. For 2026, we'll drive 25% HC savings through AI-powered automation of key workflows including SM operations, pitch development, campaign storytelling, and production. This includes SMITH (Solution Management Intelligent Tool Hub) for automated project management and Via for AI-assisted creative workflows.

### Core Goal

- **(L8 - Kingpin #1091756)** Drive 25% productivity gains by Q4 2026 through AI-powered automation of key workflows (SM operations, pitch development, campaign storytelling, production) and four strategic initiatives (knowledge management, content generation, briefing systems, resource optimization). [Owners: mackdunc, shwebs, abiteau, aleckunk]

### Team Goals

- **(Tool Adoption - Owner: Hanna / Alec)** Achieve and sustain 90% SMITH adoption from SMs, 80% Via adoption from Art Directors, Copy Writers, and Creative Directors. Measured by 90% SMITH and 80% Via adoption by Q3, sustained through Q4.
- **(Automate QA - Owner: Mirko)** Reduce total hours spent on campaign QA by 30% by EOY'26 by building a Campaign Quality Engine that prevents defects upstream, before QA starts, and makes verification largely automatic. The initiative focuses on embedding quality controls earlier in the campaign lifecycle and leveraging automation and AI-driven optimization mechanisms to proactively detect issues, standardize checks, and reduce manual effort.
- **(OBA Innovation - Owner: Christine)** Drive AI-powered automation of OBA key workflows (campaign mock development, production specifications, compliance review) and launch 2 OBA campaigns with AI-powered interactive elements that transform physical packages into memorable brand moments. Success measured by: (1) 25% reduction in campaign manual work through automated workflows, and (2) 2 interactive campaign launches featuring AI-powered personalization or dynamic elements by EOY'26.

## 3. Design Platform & Scaling

The 2025 Webflow pilot exceeded expectations -- designers averaged 50 hours per page versus 110 hours for DT-coded pages (55% reduction). For 2026, we're transitioning from pilot to production-ready architecture, enabling 100+ BIL designers to independently publish hundreds of interactive campaigns at scale while unifying our fragmented component ecosystem.

### BIL-E Partner Goals (TEX Owners: Kasey, Giulia)

- **(Kingpin #1091686)** Transition to Production-grade Webflow hosting: Replace manual export workflows with automated end-to-end deployments from Webflow Designer to Brand Stores via Webflow Custom Hosting (WCH) and AWS infrastructure. GA launch by 3/31/2026. [Owner: nielsboe]
- **(Kingpin #1091735)** Transition away from the legacy component system Halfpipe into a reusable solution: Transform BIL's component ecosystem from fragmented legacy systems to a unified, platform-agnostic architecture. Transition all 63 active Halfpipe components to the new solution, AWLS, or deprecation. [Owner: nielsboe]
- **(Kingpin #1108007)** Launch platform-agnostic component library: Unify component reuse across Webflow, custom coded landing pages, Fire TV, and self-service widgets. Drive adoption across platforms by 9/30/2026. [Owner: nielsboe]

## 4. Quality & Testing

Complex interactivity in Lighthouse campaigns has shown variable engagement rates. Systematic Weblab testing ensures data-driven decisions and reduces post-launch risk by catching issues before full deployment.

### Team Goal

- **(Reduce Bounce Rates - Owner: Kingyin)** Reduce bounce rate by at least 20% in 8 campaigns or more through Weblab testing.

## 5. Communication & Knowledge Sharing

TEX operates globally across US, EU, CA, JP, AU, and MENA with historically varying communication practices. Standardizing information flow enables cross-pod idea sharing, alignment, and collective impact -- especially critical as we support emerging work from Sponsorships, PVXD, Twitch, and Alexa+ -- and reduces potential overlap with Principal DTs.

### Team Goals

- **(Communication - Owner: Fitz)** By Q2 2026, establish standardized TEX communication mechanisms that reduce regional silos and increase cross-TEX and BIL collaboration, measured by: (1) a "reintroduction" presentation from TEX to BIL (aka TEX Narrative); (2) creating high polish bi-annual TEX-to-BIL presentations (May and October); (3) addition of Prototypes section to team WBRs; and (4) integration of PARC's prototypes to BIL's suite of AI tools (Smith, Via) via MCP.
- **(Centralize Global OBA Operations and Pilot EU Test Campaigns - Owner: Christine)** Standardize OBA operations across MENA and JP markets by Q4 2026 through unified tooling, measurement, reporting, and SOPs, building on the Mexico centralization completed in Q1'26. Pilot EU OBA program launch by Q4 2026. Success measured by: (1) completing MENA and JP standardization by Q4 2026, (2) establishing EU market feasibility assessment and stakeholder alignment by Q2 2026, and (3) launching EU pilot program with at least 1 campaign by EOY'26.
